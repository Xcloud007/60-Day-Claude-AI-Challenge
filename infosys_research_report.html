Investment verdict

Bull case

AI-led services boom, strong deal pipeline ($3.8B TCV), record FCF, expanding margins via Project Maximus, Anthropic partnership for regulated AI, ROE above 30%, attractive valuation at 15–16x P/E.

Bear case

Low single-digit revenue growth (3–4% CC), threat of AI disrupting traditional IT services, macro headwinds in US/Europe, visa policy risks, rising competition from TCS, Wipro, Accenture, and new AI-native firms.

Suitable for

Long-term investors and dividend seekers. Not ideal for short-term traders. FY26 guidance of 3–3.5% CC growth signals stability, not explosive returns.
